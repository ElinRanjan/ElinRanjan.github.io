In March 2026, I presented my research, "Quantum Theory of Molecular Phase Space: A Symmetry-Centric Molecular Quantum Computing Approach," at the Telluride workshop Quantum Computing for Quantum Chemistry, Molecular Dynamics, and Beyond. My work explores how molecular symmetries can be leveraged to develop quantum information frameworks for molecular systems, connecting concepts from quantum computing, molecular spectroscopy, and quantum chemistry. The workshop provided an opportunity to discuss these ideas with researchers working on quantum algorithms, molecular dynamics, and quantum simulation.
